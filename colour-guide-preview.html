Colour Guide
Before the system listens

How your voice
becomes colour

The colour is not a score. It is a soft translation of pitch, brightness, duration, and texture.

Pink
Warm

Close, tender, low-mid voices. Personal memories, affection, and soft nostalgia.

Yellow
Energetic

Rhythmic, lively, bright movement. A memory with pace, joy, or social energy.

Blue
Crystalline

Clear, airy, separated tones. Calm recall, distance, water, breeze, and quiet focus.

White
Bright

High, light, or sparse sound. Silence, clarity, reflection, and delicate fragments.

Your result may shift slightly each time. The system keeps a little uncertainty, like memory itself.
