Interactive light installation
Echo
Along the Shore
Echo Along the Shore is an interactive light installation that allows you to listen to other people's stories and leave your own feelings or thoughts. You can record any sound, and the system will analyze your audio to determine the corresponding color, generating a light sphere of that color. You can then drag the light sphere into the Memory Tree to illuminate your memories.

Colour Guide
Before the system listens

How your voice
becomes colour

The colour is not a score. It is a soft translation of pitch, brightness, duration, and texture.

Pink
Warm

Close, tender voices. Personal memories, affection, and soft nostalgia.

Yellow
Energetic

Rhythmic, lively sound. A memory with pace, joy, or social energy.

Blue
Crystalline

Clear, airy tones. Calm recall, distance, water, breeze, and quiet focus.

White
Bright

High, light, or sparse sound. Silence, clarity, reflection, and delicate fragments.

Your result may shift slightly each time. The system keeps a little uncertainty, like memory itself.
